About Me

I am an enthusiastic health promotion undergraduate student working towards improving community well-being through knowledge, skills, and health facilitation. My research interests focus on suicide prevention among youth, with an emphasis on enhancing their psychological well-being. I am a dedicated learner, committed to continuously expanding my knowledge and skills in the field of health promotion.

During my academic journey, I've had the opportunity to work extensively with the community of Mahakanadarawa Yaya 3, leading initiatives to improve health and well-being. I collaborated with my group to engage with key institutions, including local schools, preschools, community societies, and villagers to implement participatory approaches that address health concerns and empower community members for sustainable change.
